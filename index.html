What is good design?

I feel like a good design is something that conveys what it wants to convey in a effective and visually appealing way. I think conveying infromation effectively involves communicating your message in a concise and/or entertaining way. I feel like this is important because it makes your audience want to read your message. I feel like making your design visually appealing is important because it makes you audience want to look at your design.

I think a good example of this is NFL TV scoreboards like the one below. It conveys all the information you want to know, such as the score and the gameclock, in a concise and easy to read way. I think the design of the score board is visually appealing because it makes you want to look at it by not being overly complex or hard to read.

I think another good example of a good design is the Citroen 2CV ad below. It conveys its message that the car is a nice everyday driver in a comedic way, while also telling you information about the car, such as its price and storage space. The ad also makes you want to look at it because of the seemingly nonsense statements and "cooler" cars on it.

I think another good example of a good design is the Rstudio interface. Again like the NFL scoreboard it conveys all the information you want to know, such as your code and the objects in the environment, in a concise and easy to read way. I think the design is also visually appealing because alll of the icons and negative space.
